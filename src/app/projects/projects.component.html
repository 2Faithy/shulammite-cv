My Projects

Over the years, I’ve worked on diverse projects that combine creativity, functionality, and cutting-edge technology. Here are some of the highlights that reflect my passion for innovation and problem-solving.

  • Layken Travel Assistant Chatbot

    The Layken Travel Assistant Chatbot is a web-based conversational agent designed to enhance the travel planning experience. Built with advanced interactivity and a user-centric design, this chatbot serves as a virtual travel guide, offering personalized recommendations, real-time assistance, and seamless navigation for travelers. The chatbot is engineered for intuitive usability and aims to provide accurate, timely, and context-aware responses to user queries.

  • Citizens Solution App: A Platform for Incident Reporting and Management

    The Citizens Solution App is a web-based application designed to empower citizens to report, track, and manage incidents in their community effectively. The platform leverages modern web technologies to provide a user-friendly interface where individuals can log concerns such as public safety issues, infrastructure problems, or emergencies. By integrating geolocation services, real-time updates, and secure authentication, the app bridges the gap between citizens and authorities, fostering proactive and transparent problem resolution.
